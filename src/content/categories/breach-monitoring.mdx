> **TL;DR.** Sign up for Have I Been Pwned notifications on every email you use. Free, run by Troy Hunt, trusted by browsers and password managers alike. You will get an email when your address appears in a new dump; you change the password, you move on.

## What this category protects

The gap between "breach happens" and "you do something about it." Attackers sell credential dumps on forums within days of a breach. The average defender finds out months or years later. Free monitoring closes that gap for almost no effort.

## Hero pick: Have I Been Pwned (HIBP)

Free for individuals. Register each email you use; HIBP emails you when a dump containing your address hits the database. Domain-level monitoring is also free for domain owners. What you give up: Troy Hunt sees the hash of your email. That is it.

## Alternatives

**Firefox Monitor** — Mozilla's wrapper around HIBP, nicely integrated in Firefox. Same data source, slightly different UI.

**Proton Sentinel / Dark Web Monitoring** — Paid (Proton Unlimited plan). Adds phone-number and credit-card monitoring on top of HIBP coverage.

**DeHashed** — Paid research-grade service. Breach data plus forum leaks plus pastes. Use if you need investigator-level access to raw dumps.
