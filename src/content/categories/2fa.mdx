> **TL;DR.** Use Aegis for TOTP codes on Android, 2FAS on iOS, or Ente Auth cross-platform. Add a YubiKey (or two) for your email, password manager, and exchange accounts. Avoid SMS 2FA on anything that matters.

## What this category protects

Account takeover when a password leaks. Reusing passwords is still common, breaches are routine, and a second factor closes almost all of the damage when one happens. SMS is weak because SIM-swap attacks are cheap and real.

## Hero pick: Aegis Authenticator

Android, fully open-source, encrypted local vault, export to plain JSON for backups. No cloud, no account, no lock-in. What you give up: Android only. On iOS, Raivo is abandoned; use 2FAS or Ente Auth instead.

## Alternatives

**2FAS** — iOS and Android, optional encrypted iCloud/Google-Drive backup. Good for people who want cross-device sync without setting up their own backup strategy.

**Ente Auth** — Same company as Ente Photos. Cross-platform, end-to-end encrypted sync, free. Clean app on every OS.

**YubiKey (or Nitrokey)** — Hardware FIDO2 key. Buy two (one primary, one in a safe). Use them for irreversible accounts: email, password manager, exchange, domain registrar.
