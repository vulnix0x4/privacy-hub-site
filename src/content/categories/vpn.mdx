## What a VPN does not do

It does not stop canvas fingerprinting. It does not hide you from a site you're logged into. It does not encrypt your DNS unless you also turn DNS-over-HTTPS on in the VPN client. If the client's DNS leaks, you bought a slower pipe, not privacy. See the DNS leak test on the scanner page.
