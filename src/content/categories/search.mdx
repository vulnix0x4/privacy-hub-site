> **TL;DR.** Use Kagi. $10/month, no ads, no tracking, genuinely better results than Google for most technical queries, and they own a real crawler (Teclis). You pay with money instead of data.

## What this category protects

Your query log. Google retains searches indefinitely, links them to your account, and uses them to build the ad-targeting profile that feeds every Search and YouTube ad you see. Every query you make contributes to that model.

## Hero pick: Kagi

Paid search. No ads, so no incentive to optimize results for time-on-page. Custom weighting lets you downrank the AI-slop farms that Google rewards. What you give up: $10/month. And if you go back to an ad-supported engine, everything you typed at Kagi still stayed private, but the queries you type at the next engine won't.

## Alternatives

**Brave Search** — Free, independent index (not a Bing proxy). Fastest private option, though result quality trails Kagi for niche queries. No account required.

**DuckDuckGo** — Proxies Bing's index and serves sponsored results. Honest about it, no personalization, solid default for people who will not pay. Watch for the occasional Bing content quirk.

**Startpage** — Literally proxies Google, strips the tracking. Quality is Google quality because it is Google. Dutch company, audited.
