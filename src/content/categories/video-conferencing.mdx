> **TL;DR.** Use Jitsi Meet. Zero account, free, hosted at meet.jit.si or self-hosted. Works in any browser. Fine for 1-10 person calls; quality drops off above that.

## What this category protects

Call content from third-party enterprise dashboards, AI-transcript pipelines, and ad-graph enrichment. Zoom "AI Companion" reads your call. Teams integrates with Microsoft Copilot. Meet integrates with Workspace. Each of those is a separate model being fed your words.

## Hero pick: Jitsi Meet

Open-source, free, browser-based (no app install). Self-host for serious use (it's a docker-compose away). Quality is excellent up to ~10 participants. What you give up: no gallery of 50 faces, no built-in recording, and the free public instance sometimes has capacity issues during business hours.

## Alternatives

**Signal calls** — 1:1 or small group, end-to-end encrypted via Signal Protocol. Up to 40 participants on a call. Best for groups that are already on Signal.

**Whereby** — Norwegian, persistent meeting rooms with short URLs, free tier for 1:1. Good for recurring meetings without scheduling friction.

**Proton Meet** — Newer (released 2024), integrates with Proton Mail and Calendar. End-to-end encrypted. Pick if you already pay Proton.
