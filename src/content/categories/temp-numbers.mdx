> **TL;DR.** Use SMS-Pool for one-off verification codes. $0.10-$0.30 per code, pay in crypto or card, no account linked to your identity. For a longer-term number you actually answer on, use MySudo (US/CA) or Hushed.

## What this category protects

Your real phone number from service databases, data brokers, SIM-swap attempts, and leaks. A number given to Discord in 2017 can resurface in a 2024 breach. Every service wants SMS for "security"; most of them turn it into a tracking identifier.

## Hero pick: SMS-Pool

Rental per-message. Choose country, service, receive code, done. No account with the service keeps your real number. What you give up: SMS-Pool numbers are shared pool numbers, so they do not work for services that fingerprint reused numbers (banks, Signal).

## Alternatives

**MySudo** — Long-lived secondary numbers with voice and SMS. US and CA only. Subscription, privacy-focused company.

**Hushed** — Similar to MySudo, international coverage, per-number pricing.

**Google Voice** — Free in the US, ties to a Google account. Good for Craigslist-class use where you actually need to answer calls, not for anonymity.
