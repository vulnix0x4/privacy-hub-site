> **TL;DR.** Do the opt-outs yourself with Yael Grauer's "Big Ass Data Broker Opt-Out List" (easy-opt-outs). Free, canonical, updated. Pay a removal service only if your time is worth more than the one-weekend DIY cost.

## What this category protects

Every public-records scrape that ends up on WhitePages, Spokeo, BeenVerified, Intelius, Radaris, MyLife, and another 100 clones. These sites publish your address, age, relatives, and sometimes partial SSN. Opt-outs work; they just have to be filed at every site individually.

## Hero pick: Easy Opt-Outs (DIY)

Yael Grauer's free list at easyoptouts.com and inteltechniques.com. Step-by-step instructions per site. Weekend project. What you give up: a weekend. Realistically a second weekend six months later, because the brokers re-ingest after a while.

## Alternatives

**DeleteMe** — Paid removal service, ~$130/year, targets the biggest brokers. Genuinely removes listings; you get a quarterly report.

**Kanary** — Competitor to DeleteMe. Similar coverage, sometimes better pricing promotions.

**Incogni** — Surfshark's offering. Bundled with Surfshark VPN if you already pay for it. Wide broker list.
