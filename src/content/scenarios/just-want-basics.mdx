> **TL;DR.** You are not under active surveillance. You just want to leak less. This playlist is ordered by payoff per minute of effort. Do them top to bottom, stop when you are tired, and you will already be ahead of almost everyone.

## How to work through this

Skim each linked page's TL;DR. If you already do the thing, skip it. If it looks like a lot of work, stop at whatever level is not a lot of work. Privacy compounds. You do not need to finish the list today.

## What this won't protect you from

A targeted adversary with your real name. Law enforcement with a warrant. Someone with physical access to your unlocked laptop. If any of those describe your situation, pick a more specific scenario from the index. The list is there for a reason.
