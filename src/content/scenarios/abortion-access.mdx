> **TL;DR.** Call the Repro Legal Helpline at 1-844-868-2812 first. They are free, they are confidential, they know your state's specifics. The rest of this list is general hygiene to reduce the digital footprint of whatever you decide to do; it does not replace legal advice.

## Threat picture

Prosecutions so far have relied on Facebook Messenger logs, Google search history, and unencrypted SMS. At least two US prosecutions have used period-tracker data. The threat is prosecutorial subpoena of the big tech companies, plus civil bounty actions in states that allow them. The goal is to not be the easy case.

## What you get from this playlist

A searching-and-coordinating workflow that leaves minimal subpoena-able evidence: encrypted messaging that does not log content, search history that does not link back to a Google account, encrypted email between you and the provider.

## Notes from real people

Repro advocates say the hardest advice to follow is to not use your personal phone for the research phase. A public-library computer, a clean burner, or a trusted friend's device for the searches alone removes the biggest evidentiary source.

## What we don't cover here

Medical or legal specifics. Plan C (https://www.plancpills.org) covers medication access; If/When/How runs the Repro Legal Helpline. This site does not know your state's law. They do.
